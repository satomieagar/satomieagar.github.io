My favorite beverage is Dalgona coffee. Dalgona coffe is a whipped coffee beverage that gained popularity during the COVID-19 pandemic. It originated in South Korea and is made by vigorously whipping equal parts of instant coffee, sugar, and hot water until it becomes creamy and frothy. The whipped coffee is then spooned over a glass of milk and can be mixed or left layered. Dalgona coffee is known for its visually appealing presentation and can be customized with toppings or flavors. It offers a sweet and creamy coffee experience that can be enjoyed at home with just a few basic ingredients.
